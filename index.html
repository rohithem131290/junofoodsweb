About Us

The conecpt of time wheel, sounds fascinating to us. We believe in it. What used to be the common norm of the past, is now a USP of today. With known adverse effects of packaged food, the society as a whole is becoming conscious about it's food choices, extended to the choice of choosing quality over convenience, choice of health over taste, choice of value over money.

We at Juno, a venture started by a lovely couple, wanted to inculcate these choices in our lives, leveraging our family backgournd and culture, and create a product solution which could perfectly fit well in that sweet spot capturing all the essential values, wherein we "DON'T" have to make a "choice", because it's no longer "quality" OR "convenience", it's "everything - quality AND convenience".

Inspired by the traditional chakki, we tapped into a more sustainable and rich way to produce flours. We grind the entire grain kernel at low temperatures (cold pressed) to retain all the vitamins & fibre goodness to give you the most nourishing flours. Our whole grain stone-milled atta exceeds processed alternatives in terms of flavour and nutrition, thus giving you the most nourishing flour-ever. Try to believe it!

What is so special about our products?

It's not what we do that makes us special, rather it's "How" we do it.

  • Freshly Milled Grains

    We do not stock products in our warehouse. Every grain is freshly milled using traditional stone milling technique at low temperatures (cold-pressed) to retain nutrients and delivered promptly to you to ensure peak quality.

  • No Added Preservatives

    We do make sure that all our products are chemical-free. We do not add any artificial flavours, ingredients, or preservatives. This helps us with delivering the natural taste and health benefits to your staple diet.

  • Customised Flours

    Our products are made to order, which guarantees that your Juno experience is going to be personalised like non other. Don’t be shy and state your preferences
