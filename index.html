About Us

The newest isn't always the best way. Inspired by the traditional chakki, we tapped into a more sustainable and rich way to produce flours. We grind the entire grain kernel to retain all the vitamins & fibre goodness to give you the most nourishing flours. Our whole grain stone-milled atta exceeds processed alternatives in terms of flavour and nutrition, thus giving you the most nourishing flour-ever. Try to believe it!

  • Freshly Milled Grains

    We do not stock products in our warehouse. Every grain is freshly milled using traditional stone milling technique at low temperatures (cold-pressed) to retain nutrients and delivered promptly to you to ensure peak quality.

  • No Added Preservatives

    We do make sure that all our products are chemical-free. We do not add any artificial flavours, ingredients, or preservatives. This helps us with delivering the natural taste and health benefits to your staple diet.
